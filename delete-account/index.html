Platoon Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Platoon account and associated data, email support@platoonapp.com.

How to request deletion

Send an email to support@platoonapp.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your Platoon account.

What will be deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we will remove your profile and account information, messages, trade activity and listings, and schedule-related user data associated with your account.

Retention

Deletion requests are processed within 7 days. Limited security and audit logs may be retained for up to 30 days before deletion.

Some records may be retained longer if required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.
