Platform

One place for union communication and day-to-day operations.

Platoon is designed around the way firefighter locals actually coordinate: members need fast mobile access, while leadership needs structured tools for reaching the right people and tracking what matters.

Operational context

Two-sided value

Clear for members. Useful for leadership.

For members

  • Know what is happening across the local, station, and platoon.
  • Respond to polls and official votes from a mobile-first workflow.
  • Find documents, recommendations, messages, events, and trades quickly.

For union leaders

  • Send announcements by group, station, platoon, or membership audience.
  • Coordinate meetings, ballots, recommendations, and member input.
  • Use the admin dashboard to see recent communications and upcoming work.
